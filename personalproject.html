    Felix Tapes is an Amsterdam based DJ/Producer who has been playing his favourite records in multiple nightclubs in and around Amsterdam since 2010. Being raised by two Africa lovers, African music influenced him since his childhood. Felix also travelled through South America a lot. Just listening to his sets and productions tells you how much the rhythms and grooves from these continents inspired him to create his own style. From Nigerian funk to Amazon tribe music, everything is possible. With his percussive and euphoric sounds he already rocked dancefloors at venues like: Ruigoord, Sugar Factory, Bitterzoet, Georgies Wundergarten, Studio K, Dhoem Dhaam, Pllek and Blijburg.

    MIXTAPES

    Summer Afrofunk Mix

    This mix contains a selection of traditional West-African songs from the 80s, aswell as some trakcs of modern fusion-bands like "The Souljazz Orchestra". Expect a energetic and danceable mix of traditional and modern afrobeat/afrofunk songs!

    Downtempo Worldmusic Groove

    This one is for people who like to groove and chill on some downtempo tunes. If you have never listened to this type of music it's about time to experience the psychedelic sounds from the indigenous people of the Andes. Welcome to the world of Voodoo, Cumbia and Ayahuasca!

    Georgies Wundergarten

    A liverecording from Georgies Wundergarten 2016. I started this set at an empty stage around 14:00 and ended with hundreds of people in front of me 3 hours after. The sets start with 90BPM and ends with 130BPM, in between you can hear Latin, Soul, Funk, House and even some Hiphop
